# Privacy Policy for BounceTap

Last updated: February 2026

At BounceTap, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection
BounceTap is a minimalist arcade game. We do NOT collect any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

## 2. Game Data
Your high scores are stored locally on your device. We do not synchronize this data with any external servers.

## 3. Permissions
The application may request INTERNET permission solely for the purpose of communicating with AI services (if applicable) and basic crash reporting. No personal data is transmitted.

## 4. Third-Party Services
BounceTap may use Google Play Services for content rating and distribution. These services may collect information as per their own privacy policies.

## 5. Children's Privacy
BounceTap does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@bouncetap.app.
